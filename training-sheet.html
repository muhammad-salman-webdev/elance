Elance's DEI Basics Trainings

E-learning
  • Completed Individually
  • Available in french or english
  • 15 video capsules ( 2 to 6 minutes each )
  • Templates, guides, and dedicated customer support
  • Also includes follow-up emails, learning plans, and a weekly progress report.

Course overview

Chapter 1: The Basics of DEI

  • Objective:

    Understand the characteristics of an inclusive environment to contribute to it. Understand the true meaning of diversity, equity, and inclusion and how they fit into our workplaces.

  • What is a safe space?
  • Definitions of DEI and what it means in our daily work environment
  • Support tools:

    A list of key definitions and a tool on what to do and what not to do at work (helping attitudes).

Get a free trial

Chapter 2 : Unconscious Biases

  • Objective:

    Understand the impact of our unconscious biases in the workplace and learn how to act on important unconscious biases.

  • What are unconscious biases?
  • How can you act on your unconscious biases?
  • Bonus: "No More Walking on Eggshells" capsule
  • Support tools:

    List of possible unconscious biases and an exercise sheet for deconstructing biases.

Chapter 3: Privileges

  • Objective:

    Understand the impact of privileges in our workplaces and learn how to use positionality to ensure an inclusive environment. We also support employees in navigating potential emotions that may arise.

  • What are privileges and why are they important?
  • How to position yourself in the presence of privilege to facilitate an inclusive environment.
  • Support tools:

    A list of privileges explaining the concept of positionality with more examples, and an exercise sheet to help you position yourself in relation to others by considering your privileges.
